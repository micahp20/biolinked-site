Move without pain, lose fat without sagging, build muscle, sleep, and age well — organized into four cycles.

Your goals, in your words: reduce inflammation and move your knees, back, and hips without pain; lose fat without the skin sagging or wrinkling; and build and hold onto muscle. Cycle 1 — Fat Loss + Muscle + Energy is your daily anchor. CJC-1295 / Ipamorelin builds the overnight growth-hormone pulse — muscle, skin/collagen, and deeper sleep in one. MOTS-c lifts cellular energy and fat-burning. And Retatrutide drives the fat loss.

Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, One Vial at a Time

Dry vials: refrigerate at 36–46°F, protected from light. Never freeze.

After mixing: refrigerate and use within ~30 days. Reconstitute one vial at a time — open the next only when the current one runs dry. KLOW turns clear blue once mixed (that's the copper in GHK-Cu) — normal.

Reconstitution amounts: Reta 3 mL, KPV 1 mL, KLOW 3 mL, CJC 2 mL, MOTS 3 mL, Pinealon 1 mL, Epithalon 3 mL, SS-31 2 mL, DSIP 1 mL, Selank 1 mL, Kisspeptin 1 mL, Oxytocin 10 mL, PT-141 1 mL. Add the water slowly down the vial wall; swirl, don't shake.

Every shot: fresh single-use syringe, wipe the stopper and your skin with alcohol, rotate injection sites.

Execution & Tips
Read Before Day 1
Read This First — Because of Your History
Reta: Low, Slow, and We Don't Push Through Nausea

You had very pervasive vomiting on Semaglutide / Tirzepatide. Reta is the same class, so we're treating it with respect: you're starting at 10u (~1.33 mg) once a week — well below a normal starting dose. Many people who couldn't tolerate Sema/Tirz do fine on a low, slow Reta ramp, but we go carefully.

The rules:

· Message Us after each of your first few Monday doses so we can adjust before anything builds.
· Monday + Tuesday: small, low-fat meals, stop at ~80% full, hydrate hard (2–3 L/day).
· If the nausea or vomiting comes back — hold the next dose, don't push through. We'd rather drop to a half-dose (5u) or pause than have you sick.

Allergy note: your reactions to prescription pain meds don't apply to these peptides (none are opioids or NSAIDs), but keep any pharmacy anti-nausea meds you already tolerate on hand just in case. Flag the sulfa and B-12 sensitivities with any provider before adding anything new.

AM Block + PM Pre-Bed, Cycle by Cycle

AM (fasted): KPV + KLOW + MOTS + Kisspeptin every weekday, plus Oxytocin M/W/F and Reta on Monday. Wait 30–45 min before eating.

Tip 02 — Injection Refresher
SubQ, Small Needle — You've Done This

All SubQ, short insulin needle. Wash hands, wipe the stopper and skin with alcohol, pull to the right unit mark, pinch an inch of skin, insert at 45°, push slowly, drop the syringe in the sharps container. Rotate sites.

Draw marks: Reta 10u, KPV 10u, KLOW 15u, CJC/Ipa 10u, MOTS 10u, Kisspeptin 10u, Oxytocin 10u, DSIP 10u, Selank 10u, PT-141 10u (5u first use), Pinealon 20u, Epithalon 15u, SS-31 10u. Glance at the line every shot.

Tip 03 — Week-by-Week
What to Expect

Weeks 1–2: KLOW + KPV start taking the edge off joint stiffness/inflammation — often the first thing you notice. CJC/Ipa deepens sleep within the first week (big for your 1/5). Reta is intentionally gentle — go slow, watch tolerance. Don't expect scale movement yet.

Weeks 3–5: joints move easier, energy from MOTS is established, and fat loss begins as Reta settles in (assuming it's tolerated). Skin stays supported by the GHK-Cu/collagen work so loss doesn't leave you saggy. Kisspeptin/Oxytocin smooth out mood and stress.

Weeks 6–8: the recomposition window — leaner while holding muscle from the GH pulse + your training. Reorder KPV/KLOW/MOTS/CJC as they empty. Run the 20-day Cycle 2 longevity course whenever it fits (great overlap with any sleep push, since Epithalon helps there too).

Ongoing: this is a run-till-empty, cycle-by-cycle protocol. We titrate Reta up only as tolerated, and keep what's working.

Tip 04 — Sleep + Stress
Your 1/5 Sleep and 4/5 Stress Are a Priority

Sleep is where the muscle, skin, and fat-loss work actually happens — so fixing the 1/5 is a real lever, not a side quest. CJC/Ipamorelin deepens sleep nightly; DSIP is your as-needed heavy hitter for rough nights; Epithalon (during the 20-day course) helps reset the melatonin rhythm. Keep the magnesium glycinate you already take in the evening — it stacks well here.

For the 4/5 stress: Selank on wound-up days and Oxytocin M/W/F both bring the stress response down without sedation. Between the peptides and better sleep, the stress number should start moving within a few weeks.

Tip 05 — Red Flags
When to Pause & Message Us

Hold Reta if: vomiting returns, persistent nausea >24 hrs, or severe abdominal pain radiating to the back. This is the one to watch given your history.

Injection sites: a little redness at the pinhole is normal; spreading redness, warmth, swelling, or pus that's worsening = possible infection — stop that site and message me.

PT-141 first use: half dose (5u), and expect possible mild nausea/flushing — that's normal and passes.

Anything that feels off, message first — don't push through, and don't restart on your own. We adjust based on what's actually happening.
