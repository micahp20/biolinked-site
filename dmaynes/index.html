Reconstitution (80-unit vial — 2 mL BAC water)
  1. Draw 2 mL of bacteriostatic water into a fresh insulin syringe and inject slowly down the vial wall — never squirt directly on the powder
  2. Gently swirl the vial (don't shake) until the powder is fully dissolved — solution should be clear

  5. Refrigerate after reconstitution — good for 30 days. Yields ~20 doses per vial

Storage & Handling

Before reconstitution (dry powder)

Store all vials in the refrigerator at 36–46°F (2–8°C). Keep away from light and moisture. Do not freeze. Shelf life is typically 12–24 months unopened.

After reconstitution

All reconstituted vials must be refrigerated. Use within 30 days. Never freeze a reconstituted peptide. Always use a fresh insulin syringe for each draw — never reuse.

Bacteriostatic water (BAC water)

Use sterile BAC water for all four compounds. BAC water's benzyl alcohol preservative extends shelf life of reconstituted peptides. Do not substitute plain sterile water.

Syringes & injection sites

Use 29–31 gauge insulin syringes (0.5 mL or 1 mL). Never reuse. Dispose in a sharps container. Rotate sites daily — abdomen (left/right of navel) and outer thigh both work well.

What to Monitor
Normal: Minor injection site redness or small bruise — rotate sites daily. Resolves within 24 hours.
Normal: Vivid dreams or slightly deeper sleep in the first 1–2 weeks — CJC/Ipamorelin is amplifying your GH pulse during sleep. This is a good sign.
Normal: Mild facial flushing or warmth 30–60 minutes after PT-141 — transient and typically resolves within 1–2 hours. Very common in first-time users.
Normal: Mild water retention in weeks 1–2 as GH output increases — this resolves as the body adapts and is not fat gain.
Monitor: Nausea after PT-141 — reduce to half dose (5 units / 250 mcg) and contact BioLinked if it persists beyond first use.
Monitor: Persistent bloating or GI discomfort beyond week 2 — may indicate KLOW dose needs adjustment. Reach out to your BioLinked consultant.
Monitor: Injection site hot, swollen, or red beyond 24 hours — potential technique issue or contamination. Do not reuse that vial and contact BioLinked immediately.
