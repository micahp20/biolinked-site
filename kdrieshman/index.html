Protocol Overview — The Khavinson Pineal Reset

A focused longevity reset built around a single biological axis — the pineal gland and the brain it talks to — with a seasonal melanin builder and a sleep-architecture bonus alongside it.

Epitalon + Pinealon — The Khavinson Pairing

Epitalon is a pineal-targeting tetrapeptide that activates telomerase, restores circadian melatonin rhythm, and modulates the hypothalamic–pituitary axis at the level it was designed to be regulated from. Pinealon is its tripeptide complement — same family, different angle: neuroprotection, cognitive resilience, and direct support of the brain tissue downstream of the pineal signal. Run together for 20 consecutive days, this is the canonical Khavinson protocol — Epitalon resets the upstream signal, Pinealon protects the structures receiving it. Twenty days isn't arbitrary. Bioregulator effects accumulate; the 20-day window is what the Russian clinical literature settled on as the dose-response sweet spot before diminishing returns.

Melanotan 1 — UV Protection Ahead of Exposure

Melanin is your cellular sunscreen, and the body builds it slowly. MT-1 is an alpha-MSH analog that signals melanocytes to lay down eumelanin (the protective pigment, not the red-orange pheomelanin) before UV starts hitting your skin in volume. The trick is timing: start now, before peak sun season — by the time exposure ramps up, your protection is already in place. Twice-weekly dosing (Mon/Thu) is the standard maintenance cadence once baseline pigmentation is established. It doesn't synergize with the bioregulators directly, but it belongs in a longevity stack — UV damage is one of the largest external accelerants of cellular aging.

DSIP — Bonus Inclusion for Sleep Architecture

Delta Sleep-Inducing Peptide deepens slow-wave sleep without the next-day grogginess of pharmaceutical sleep aids. It pairs naturally with Epitalon since both touch circadian rhythm, but DSIP works on a different mechanism — modulating the delta-wave generators directly. Use it as needed on disrupted nights, not nightly. Included as a complimentary bottle to round out the stack.

How it fits together. Epitalon resets the pineal signal. Pinealon protects the brain receiving it. DSIP backs up the sleep architecture both compounds support. MT-1 sits parallel — protecting the body from the largest external aging input (UV) while the others work on the internal clock. Twenty-day intensives are exactly that: intensives. You don't need to redose them constantly to get the benefit.
