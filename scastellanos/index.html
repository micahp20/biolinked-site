Retatrutide — Fat Loss Lead

Storage & Handling
Keep It Clean · Rotate Sites
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Rotated

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze. All six compounds are temperature-sensitive — the fridge is non-negotiable.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days of reconstitution. Don't open vial #2 of anything until vial #1 is close to empty — keeps the 30-day clock manageable.

Syringe discipline: fresh insulin syringe for every shot. All six compounds are SubQ — no IM in this stack. Swap to a fresh needle between drawing and injecting when possible (piercing the rubber stopper dulls the tip and raises site reactions).

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen rotates between 4 quadrants (upper left/right, lower left/right). With 3–4 shots in a single AM session, spread them across all four quadrants — never the same 1″ zone twice in a session, and never the same quadrant two days in a row.
