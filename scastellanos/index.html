Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Rotated

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days. Reconstitute one vial of each compound at a time — don't start the 30-day clock on a second vial until the first is close to empty.

Syringe discipline: fresh U-100 insulin syringe for every shot. Never reuse. Wipe stopper and injection site with alcohol pad before every draw and every shot.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen rotates between 4 quadrants. Never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row.

Week-by-Week
What to Expect Across the 8 Weeks
Phase 1 — Weeks 1–3 (Reta Ramp)
Acclimation · Get the Rhythm

Day 1: reconstitute everything. Begin M–F AM shot routine with 5-Amino + KLOW + MOTS. Reta runs Monday + Thursday at 2 mg per dose (4 mg/wk) for these first 3 weeks — conservative ramp to confirm tolerance and let the GI tract acclimate cleanly.

Expect mild appetite suppression by Day 3–5 (Reta's GLP-1 kicks in fast given your Tirz history). Reduced "food noise" and stronger morning focus by end of Week 1.

This is the right window to try Selank — pick one stressful afternoon and take one dose 30–45 min before the moment, see how the calm-clarity feels.

Watch for: mild GI on Reta shot days (you already know this from Tirz), slightly looser sleep latency from MOTS — both normalize by Week 2.

Phase 2 — Weeks 4–5 (Reta Step Up)
Fat Loss Curve Takes Hold

Start of Week 4: Reta moves to M / W / F (6 mg/wk). Same 2 mg per dose, just one extra day. If Phase 1 was clean, the step-up should slot in without GI noise. If GI is still rough at the end of Week 3, hold at Mon + Thu for one more week before adding the Wednesday dose.

By Week 4–5 most of Reta's appetite suppression peaks — expect to lose 1–2 lb/week through this window. KLOW + MOTS effects start layering: workouts cost less, recovery is quicker, the mid-day energy crash gets noticeably less sharp.

Sleep check: notice whether broken sleep is improving on its own — if not, that's the moment to use Oxytocin pre-bed on rough nights (250 mcg, abdomen).

Alcohol: appetite suppression usually drops drink count organically; pay attention to whether Selank on stressful afternoons is reducing the 5 PM reach.

Phase 3 — Weeks 5–6
The Compound Effect

By end of Week 6 you should be at ~210–212 lbs (12–14 lbs off baseline) on track for the 205 target. Mood floor should be visibly higher than where you started — combined effect of Reta + better sleep + Oxytocin/Selank usage when stress peaks + just being lighter and moving more.

Golf: flexibility / rotation should feel meaningfully better — KLOW + a lighter frame compound.

Watch for: Reta plateau around Week 5–6 is common; if scale flattens, hold dose, don't escalate — the rest of the stack keeps working behind the scenes.

Phase 4 — Weeks 7–8
Final Push + Decision Point

Target finish: 205–207 lbs. This is the cleanest window of the cycle: energy is back, recovery dialed, mood stabilized, athletic capacity showing up on the course.

General Tips
Execution · Sleep · Signals
Tip 01 — Fasted Window
MOTS-c Needs an Empty Stomach

MOTS-c works through AMPK signaling, which is most active in a fasted state. Inject it first thing in the morning, before food or coffee with cream. Wait 30–45 minutes before eating. Black coffee and water are fine.

The other M–F compounds (5-Amino, KLOW) aren't fasted-strict but it's easiest to do them all in the same AM window before food.

Tip 02 — Using the PRN Tools Intentionally
Selank & Oxytocin Aren't Daily Habits

Selank is for the 3–5 PM "edge" window when stress climbs and the pull toward a drink is strongest. Take it before that moment lands, not after — 30–45 min ahead. Cap at 2 doses/day.

Oxytocin is for emotional load: before a heavy family conversation, after a bad day, pre-bed on high-stress nights. Don't use it daily — that blunts the response. Use it intentionally on the hard days.

Tip 03 — Signs the Protocol Is Working
What to Watch For

Week 1: appetite drops noticeably (Reta + GLP-1 history kicks in fast). Morning focus sharpens.

Weeks 2–3: 1–2 lb/week loss. Recovery from training noticeably faster. Less afternoon crash from 5-Amino + MOTS layering NAD+ and mitochondrial output.

Weeks 4–5: sleep quality improving on its own. Drink count organically dropping. KLOW's tissue repair effects most visible — old golf nags quieting down.

Weeks 6–8: 12–20 lbs off baseline. Mood floor visibly higher. Athletic capacity returning. This is the "feeling like yourself again" window.

Yellow flags — reassess: persistent GI past Week 2, sleep getting worse instead of better, weight loss faster than 3 lb/week (deficit too aggressive), or strength dropping noticeably in the gym (lean mass loss; increase protein, hold Reta dose).
