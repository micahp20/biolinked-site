Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Rotated

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days. Always use a fresh insulin syringe for each draw — never reuse.

BAC water + MCAS: standard bacteriostatic water contains benzyl alcohol as a preservative. If you've ever had a reaction to benzyl alcohol (it's in some IV meds and cosmetics), request preservative-free sterile water for injection (SWFI) from McGuff instead — but you'll need to reconstitute less BAC water at a time since SWFI doesn't preserve open vials. For most MCAS folks, standard BAC water is fine.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen rotates between 4 quadrants. Never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row. With 7 shots/day in Phase 1, plan your rotation pattern — e.g. AM shots in upper quadrants, PM shots in lower quadrants.

Tip 01 — MCAS / Histamine Sensitivity (Important)
Slow-Introduction Plan for Week 1

You mentioned MCAS — mast cell activation syndrome. This matters for peptide intro because mast cells can react to several mechanisms peptides touch (melanocortin pathways, GH secretagogue effects, even the bacteriostatic water itself). The risk is real but manageable. The protocol: introduce ONE new compound per day in Week 1, observe for 24 hours, then add the next. This lets us cleanly identify any reactor without confounding from multiple new compounds at once.

The phased structure IS the MCAS safety plan. Each new compound gets isolated when it's introduced — if you flare, the suspect is unambiguous.

Phase 1 intro (Wks 1–4) — low reactivity pair:

  1. Day 1: Start Pinealon AM only. Skip Epitalon.
  2. Day 2: Add Epitalon PM. Run both for the rest of Phase 1.

Phase 2 intro (Wk 5) — the higher-risk pair:

  1. Wk 5 Mon: Start Kisspeptin AM only. No PT-141 yet.
  2. Wk 5 Thu: If Kiss is clean, first PT-141 dose at half (5 units / 0.05 mL / 250 mcg) — PT-141 is the highest theoretical MCAS risk in the stack.
  3. Wk 5 Mon (next): If half-dose was clean, scale PT-141 to full 10 units (0.10 mL / 500 mcg).

Phase 3 intro (Wk 13) — gut + GH pair:

  1. Wk 13 Mon: Start BPC-157 AM only. Often MCAS-friendly (may stabilize mast cells). Skip CJC/Ipa.
  2. Wk 13 Tue: If BPC is clean, add CJC/Ipa PM. Run both for the rest of Phase 3.

What to watch for after each new compound: flushing (face/chest redness), itching, hives, GI cramping/diarrhea, sudden headache, racing heart, brain fog spike. These are mast-cell flare signs.

If a flare happens: take your usual antihistamine immediately, drop the suspect compound, message us. We can either pause that compound or try a much lower dose later.

Tip 02 — Why PT-141 + Kisspeptin for Women
The Hormonal Balance Pair (And Why It Works With Your Birth Control)

Most peptide protocols are male-focused. PT-141 and Kisspeptin-10 are notable exceptions — both have women-specific research backing, which is why they're paired in your protocol. Here's the case:

The problem they solve: hormonal birth control (your Nuvaring) gives you contraception — that part works. But it also flattens downstream effects that aren't part of the contraceptive design: libido, motivation, the felt "drive" or "spark," sometimes brain fog. Conventional medicine doesn't have great answers for these because the conventional approach is to add more hormones, which conflicts with the birth control. Both PT-141 and Kisspeptin work through non-hormonal brain pathways — so they restore those downstream effects without disrupting the contraception.

PT-141 (Bremelanotide):

  • The only FDA-approved on-demand medication for hypoactive sexual desire in premenopausal women
  • Works on MC3R/MC4R melanocortin receptors in the brain — not estrogen, not progesterone, not testosterone
  • Effects: increased desire, improved arousal, central reward + motivation activation
  • Doesn't require timing with sexual activity — Mon + Thu dosing (2×/wk) builds steady-state benefit through Phase 2

Kisspeptin-10:

  • The master regulator of the HPG (hypothalamic-pituitary-gonadal) axis
  • Recent Imperial College London studies (2017–2023) show kisspeptin improves both sexual responsiveness AND mood in women specifically — both pre- and post-menopausal
  • Works upstream — helps "wake up" the brain's natural reward circuits that hormonal contraception tends to flatten
  • Doesn't override your birth control's contraceptive effect — works on signaling, not hormone replacement

Why the pair vs. either alone: Kisspeptin is upstream (signaling), PT-141 is downstream (receptor activation). Running them together hits both ends of the same pathway, which is why women on the combo often describe a more complete "lifting" of the flatness than either compound delivers solo.

Tirz handles the weight loss part. You're going from 140 to 132 — an 8-pound drop. The scale will move and that's not the impressive part of this protocol. The impressive part will be what you look like at 132.

At your goal weight with this protocol, you'll look distinctly different than at the same weight on Tirz alone — more toned, more defined, less "small but soft."

What to measure besides the scale: waist circumference, photos in the same outfit every 2 weeks, how clothes fit (a "tight" pair of jeans is a better marker than a number). The body comp shift will be more dramatic than the 8 lbs suggests.

Tip 05 — Why Sequential, Not Concurrent
One Block at a Time — You'll Know What's Working

The biggest mistake with peptide stacks is running everything at once. You can't tell which compound is doing what, and for MCAS the layering risk multiplies. This protocol runs in 3 sequential phases so each block gets a clean look.

Phase 1 isolates anti-aging: if brain fog lifts during Wks 1–4, it's unambiguously Pinealon (+ Epitalon supporting sleep). If it doesn't lift, you know to look elsewhere — not waste 16 weeks wondering.

Phase 2 isolates hormonal: if the Nuvaring-related flatness lifts during Wks 5–12, it's the Kiss + PT-141 pair. The 8-week run gives the upstream HPG signaling time to do its work.

Phase 3 isolates gut + recomp: if bloat resolves in Wks 13–16, it's BPC-157. If muscle preservation shows in mirror photos, it's CJC/Ipa.
