Week-by-Week
What to Expect Across the 16 Weeks
Tip 01 — The 4-Week HGH Taper
Why You Don't Just Stop at Week 12

Exogenous HGH suppresses your pituitary's own GH output via negative feedback. After 12 weeks of daily 2 IU, your endogenous GH axis is dampened — not damaged, but quiet. Stopping cold from 2 IU to zero crashes the system: your own GH stays suppressed for weeks, and the body comp gains you built start unwinding while the axis catches up.

The 4-week taper walks down in three steps:

· Wks 13–14 — 1.5 IU M–F. The drop signals the pituitary to start producing again, but exogenous coverage stays above the threshold where loss accelerates.
· Wk 15 — 1.0 IU M–F. Pituitary continues to wake; your own GH starts contributing meaningfully.
· Wk 16 — 1.0 IU M / W / F only. Frequency cut to 3x/wk; transitions you to fully endogenous by week's end.
· Wk 17+ — off. Your own axis should be back to baseline within 2–3 weeks post-taper.

The taper isn't optional — it's how the stack ends without losing what you built. Don't skip steps.

Tip 02 — IM Technique for L-Carnitine
Different Syringe, Different Site, Different Sting

L-Carnitine is the only IM shot in this stack — everything else is SubQ. IM = into the muscle, deeper than SubQ. Use a 3 mL Luer-lock syringe + 25G × 1.5″ needle, NOT an insulin syringe. Sites: glute (upper outer quadrant), vastus lateralis (outer mid-thigh), or delt (top of shoulder).

Step by step:

1. Warm the vial in your hand for 60 seconds (reduces sting).
2. Wipe stopper + injection site with an alcohol pad.
3. Draw 0.85 mL into the Luer-lock syringe.
4. Swap to a fresh 25G needle if you used one to draw (sharper = less tissue damage = less sting).
5. Pinch a small amount of tissue if leaner; full needle in at 90° for glute/quad/delt.
6. Aspirate (pull back slightly) — if blood, withdraw and re-site. No blood, inject slowly: ~10 seconds for the full 0.85 mL. Slower = less sting.
7. Withdraw, gentle pressure with the alcohol pad, no rub.

Expect shots 1–4 to sting noticeably more than the SubQ shots — this is the L-carnitine, not a technique problem. Most users report the sting fades by shot 5 as the muscle adapts. If it's still significant by shot 6–7, try a different site or warm longer before drawing.

Weeks 1–2

Heavy injection cadence — 5 shots on M/W/F. Don't worry about feeling dramatic changes yet; HGH and the GH-axis compounds take 2–3 weeks to register clinical signal. What you should notice: deeper sleep within 7–10 days (the CJC/Ipa nocturnal GH amplification), mild water retention in the fingers / face (normal early HGH side effect, resolves), and the first L-Carnitine shots will sting (also normal, fades).

Lock in the daily rhythm. HGH AM fasted, CJC/Ipa pre-bed empty stomach. M/W/F: L-Carnitine pre-workout, IGF-1 LR3 post-workout with carbs. Logging dose + timing for the first 2 weeks helps you identify any spacing issues before they compound.

Weeks 3–4
First Signals

Where most users notice the first body comp shifts. Sleep is consistently deeper. Recovery between training sessions faster — soreness clears in 24 hrs vs. 48. Mild fasted body comp shifts visible in the mirror, especially around the midsection.

L-Carnitine sting should be substantially reduced by now. If still significant, see Tip 02 above for technique refinement.

Maintain protein at 1g/lb bodyweight minimum. The GH + IGF-1 amplification needs amino acid substrate to actually build/preserve muscle.

Weeks 5–8
Peak Build / Burn Window

The full stack is at steady-state. Fat loss should be visibly accelerating; lean tissue holding or building if you're lifting heavy 3x/wk minimum. This is the meaty middle of the protocol.

Monitor: morning fasted glucose if you have a meter (HGH can mildly raise it — if you're seeing >110 fasted, dial back the carb meal post-IGF or pull HGH timing earlier in the day). Hand tingling/numbness occasionally is a normal GH effect (carpal tunnel-like, fades when HGH stops). Persistent tingling = flag.

Weeks 9–10
Final Main Phase · Prep for 5-Amino Ramp

Last 2 weeks of M/W/F 5-Amino dosing before the Wk 11–12 daily ramp. Body comp curve should be steepest in this stretch as accumulated GH amplification, IGF-1 anabolism, and L-Carnitine-mediated fat oxidation all compound.

Mental note: HGH taper starts in 3 weeks. Plan supplement / training adjustments around the wind-down.

Weeks 11–12

5-Amino-1MQ goes daily M–F. Tue / Thu jump from 2 shots to 3 (HGH + 5-Amino AM, CJC/Ipa pre-bed). The bump is intentional — you're stacking maximum NNMT block + NAD+ restoration into the back end of the main phase right when HGH-mediated fat mobilization peaks.

End of Wk 12: 5-Amino, CJC/Ipa, IGF-1 LR3, L-Carnitine all wrap (vials empty or supply ends). From Wk 13 forward, it's HGH alone walking down.

Weeks 13–14
HGH Taper Step 1 — 1.5 IU M–F

HGH drops from 20u (2 IU) to 15u (1.5 IU) M–F. First step of the walk-down. Your body comp gains continue to consolidate; the lower exogenous signal lets the pituitary start waking up. Mild water shift in the first few days is normal as GH levels recalibrate; resolves within 5–7 days.

Week 15

HGH drops to 10u (1.0 IU) M–F. Endogenous GH axis is meaningfully contributing again. Sleep may dip briefly — pituitary catching up. Should resolve by end of week.

Week 16

Final step. 10u (1.0 IU) on Mon / Wed / Fri only. Tue / Thu off entirely. You're transitioning to fully endogenous GH by Wk 17.

Post-cycle assessment (Wk 17+): off all compounds. How does body comp compare to Wk 1? Strength holding? Sleep quality (endogenous GH should be back to baseline within 2–3 weeks). These answers determine whether to (a) run a maintenance cycle, (b) repeat with adjustments, or (c) take 6–8 weeks off and reassess.

Tip 03 — Reconstitution Cadence
One Vial of Each, As Each Empties

Reconstituted peptides have a 30-day fridge window. Don't recon the next vial of any compound until the current one is close to empty. Working pattern across the 16 weeks:

· HGH: recon vial #1 Day 1 (covers ~12 doses / ~2.5 weeks at 2 IU); recon #2 around Day 14; continue rolling through 7 vials across the cycle + taper.
· CJC/Ipa: recon #1 Day 1 (20 doses = 4 weeks); recon #2 around Day 28; #3 around Day 56.
· IGF-1 LR3: recon #1 Day 1 (20 doses = ~7 wks of M/W/F); recon #2 around Day 49.
· 5-Amino-1MQ: recon #1 Day 1 (25 doses = covers Wks 1-10 M/W/F = 30 doses; you'll finish vial #1 around Day 56 and recon #2 then to cover the Wk 11–12 daily ramp).
· L-Carnitine: pre-mixed, no recon. Refrigerate after first use.

Tip 04 — AM Fasted Discipline for HGH and CJC/Ipa
Food (Especially Carbs) Blunts the GH Pulse

HGH and CJC/Ipa both work via GH secretion. Insulin spike from a meal blunts the natural GH pulse — if you eat too close to either shot, you cut your dose's effectiveness substantially. Targets:

· HGH AM: empty stomach. Inject first thing after waking. Wait 30–45 min before eating. Water / black coffee OK in that window.
· CJC/Ipa pre-bed: empty stomach, 2+ hrs after last meal. If you eat at 8 PM, inject at 10 PM or later. Don't eat / drink anything except water after.

The IGF-1 LR3 post-workout shot is the opposite — pair with a carb meal (insulin amplifies IGF signal at the muscle).

Tip 05 — Hydration + Monitoring
Water Up, Watch Glucose

HGH increases water retention especially in the first 2–3 weeks. 100 oz water per day minimum helps the body distribute it without face / finger puffiness. Reduce dietary sodium during the loading phase if you're sensitive.

HGH can mildly raise fasted blood glucose. If you have a glucometer: check morning fasted weekly. <100 = ideal, 100–115 = monitor, >115 sustained = pull HGH timing earlier and reduce simple carbs around the IGF-1 LR3 shot. Most users have no glucose issues at 2 IU, but it's worth tracking.

Tip 06 — What to Monitor
Normal vs. Watch For

Normal (no action):

· Mild water retention / puffiness first 2-3 weeks of HGH — resolves
· Occasional hand tingling / numbness (carpal-tunnel feel) — mild GH effect, resolves when HGH stops
· L-Carnitine sting first 4 shots — warm vial, slow injection
· Mild injection-site redness or small bruising — rotate sites and use proper technique
· Sleep dip briefly during the HGH taper (Wk 13–15 transitions) — pituitary catching up

Monitor (contact BioLinked):

· Injection site hot, swollen, or red beyond 24 hours — technique issue or contamination
· Persistent hand tingling / numbness past Wk 4 — can happen at higher GH exposure; flag to reassess timing
· Fasted glucose >125 sustained — HGH timing/dose adjustment needed
· L-Carnitine sting still significant past shot 7 — technique or site rotation review
· Severe joint pain or swelling (especially fingers / wrists) — flag; can indicate water retention exceeding normal range
