Protocol Overview — How This Stack Works Together
HGH (Somatropin) 2 IU
The foundation of the stack, dosed at a conservative anti-aging / recovery range. Drives protein synthesis, preferentially burns stored fat as fuel, and rebuilds lean tissue. Runs M–F for 12 weeks, then walks down across a 4-week taper (1.5 → 1 → 1 M/W/F) so your pituitary wakes back up gradually instead of crashing.
CJC-1295 / Ipamorelin
A dual GH secretagogue blend that amplifies endogenous GH release via two complementary mechanisms (GHRH + ghrelin-receptor). Runs pre-bed through all 12 weeks — stacked on top of HGH during the cycle, and keeping your own GH axis awake as exogenous HGH walks down.
IGF-1 LR3
The primary downstream anabolic mediator of GH signaling. LR3's 20–30 hour half-life keeps IGF-1 receptor activity elevated through the critical post-workout protein synthesis window. Dosed M/W/F within 30 minutes of finishing training, paired with a carb meal for insulin-IGF amplification.
5-Amino-1MQ
An NNMT inhibitor that restores intracellular NAD+ in adipose tissue and muscle. HGH pulls fat out of storage at a high rate — if NAD+ is low, those liberated fatty acids recirculate instead of being oxidized. 5-Amino-1MQ keeps adipose tissue receptive to burning what HGH has mobilized, and activates dormant muscle satellite cells as reserve for rebuilding.
L-Carnitine (IM)
The transport molecule that physically shuttles fatty acids across the mitochondrial membrane. Without sufficient carnitine at the site, mobilized fat gets recirculated. Injectable delivers near-complete bioavailability versus ~15% oral — so every fat molecule HGH liberates actually reaches the mitochondria and gets burned.
Stack Synergy: HGH + CJC/Ipa create layered GH signaling that simultaneously drives protein synthesis and fat mobilization. IGF-1 LR3 amplifies the downstream anabolic response directly at the muscle cell, timed to the training window. 5-Amino-1MQ restores the NAD+ fuel needed to oxidize liberated fat at the mitochondrial level. L-Carnitine is the essential transport linking fat mobilization to fat oxidation. Each compound addresses a distinct step in the cascade — none are redundant, and each one enables the next.

Storage & Handling
Keep It Clean · Rotate Sites
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Rotated

⚠ Mixed HGH supply — keep bottles separated. Store the 24 IU bottles (Main Phase) in one clearly labeled ziplock and the 36 IU bottle (Taper) in a different part of the fridge. At Week 13 you'll switch to the 36 IU bottle and the unit marks change — do not let them end up next to each other in the same tray.

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze. HGH, CJC/Ipa, IGF-1 LR3, and 5-Amino-1MQ are temperature-sensitive — the fridge is non-negotiable.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days of reconstitution. Reconstitute one vial of each compound at a time — don't start the 30-day clock on a second vial until the first is close to empty.

L-Carnitine: pre-mixed in solution, store refrigerated once opened. Protect from light. Does not require reconstitution.

Syringe discipline: fresh insulin syringe for every SubQ shot. Fresh 3 mL Luer-lock + 25G × 1.5″ needle for every IM shot. Swap to a fresh needle between drawing and injecting when possible — piercing a rubber stopper dulls the needle tip and increases site reactions.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen rotates between 4 quadrants (upper left/right, lower left/right), never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row. IM rotates between glute left, glute right, vastus lateralis left, vastus lateralis right on a 4-site rotation.
