Protocol Overview — How This Stack Works Together
Retatrutide
The engine of fat loss. Triple agonist (GLP-1 / GIP / glucagon) suppresses appetite, accelerates fat oxidation, and improves insulin sensitivity. Titrates from 0.5 mg up to 1 mg on M / W / F — the slow ramp is what keeps GI side effects manageable.
5-Amino-1MQ
An NNMT inhibitor that restores intracellular NAD+ in fat and muscle. Reta aggressively mobilizes fat — if NAD+ is low, those liberated fatty acids recirculate instead of being oxidized. 5-Amino keeps the cellular fuel machinery primed so what Reta releases actually gets burned, and activates dormant muscle satellite cells as reserve for rebuilding.
MOTS-C
A mitochondrial-derived peptide that mimics exercise at the cellular level — activates AMPK, improves glucose disposal, and increases insulin sensitivity. Stacked alongside Reta, it amplifies metabolic efficiency and helps preserve lean tissue through an aggressive calorie deficit.
CJC-1295 / Ipamorelin
A dual GH secretagogue blend (GHRH analog + ghrelin receptor agonist) that amplifies endogenous growth hormone release. Drives recovery, deeper sleep, and lean tissue preservation — the counterweight to Reta's catabolic pressure. Pre-bed on an empty stomach is non-negotiable.
Stack Synergy: Reta does the heavy lifting on fat loss, but used alone in an aggressive deficit it can erode lean tissue and leave mobilized fat un-oxidized. 5-Amino-1MQ restores the NAD+ fuel needed to actually burn what Reta releases. MOTS-C runs metabolic protection in parallel — better glucose disposal, preserved insulin sensitivity, exercise-mimetic AMPK activation. CJC/Ipa rebuilds at night through amplified GH pulses: deeper sleep, faster recovery, lean mass held intact. Each compound addresses a distinct part of the recomp cascade — none redundant, each one enabling the next.

Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Rotated

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days. Reconstitute one vial at a time — don't start the 30-day clock on a second until the first is close to empty.

Syringe discipline: fresh U-100 insulin syringe for every shot. Never reuse.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen rotates between 4 quadrants. Never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row.
