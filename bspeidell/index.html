Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Single-Use

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F (2–8°C) before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze. Shelf life is typically 12–24 months unopened.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days for MOTS-C / Semax / Selank / SS-31. NAD+ within 14 days for best potency. Never freeze a reconstituted peptide.

Syringe discipline: fresh single-use insulin syringe for every shot. Never reuse. Wipe stopper + injection site with alcohol pad every time.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen, outer thigh, or glute. Rotate sites daily. Never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row.

Execution Tips
Fasted Window · NAD+ Technique · Pairing
Tip 01 — Critical
NAD+ Injection Technique · Go Slow

NAD+ flushing is the #1 reason people quit before they should. Inject slowly — 30 to 60 seconds, not 5. Fast injection causes the body to release histamine, which produces the warm, red, prickly flush.

First two sessions: half-dose (30 units / ~50 mg) to assess tolerance. Then move up to 100 mg full dose. If flushing is still rough at 100 mg, slow your injection further before considering reducing the dose.

Mild warmth or flushing for 5–10 minutes after the shot is normal and resolves on its own. Severe flushing (full-body, lasting > 30 minutes) means slow down further.

Tip 02 — Fasted Window
Mitochondrial Peptides Need an Empty Stomach

MOTS-C, SS-31, and NAD+ all hit hardest on a fasted morning. No food, no calories, no coffee with cream. Black coffee and water are fine. Wait 30–45 minutes after injection before eating.

Semax is also AM but doesn't strictly require fasted — stack it in the same morning window for simplicity. Selank in the afternoon (pre-stress / pre-meeting / 3–5 PM dip).

Tip 03 — The Semax / Selank Pair
Sharpen in the AM, Steady in the PM

These two are designed to work together across the day. Semax in the morning lifts BDNF and sharpens focus. Selank in the afternoon takes the edge off without sedation — ideal for the 3–5 PM stress window or pre-meeting.

Don't stack them in the same hour — you blunt the contrast between the focused-AM and steady-PM signals. Space them 4+ hours apart for best effect.
