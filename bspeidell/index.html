Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Single-Use

Dry (lyophilized) vials: store refrigerated at 36–46°F (2–8°C) before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze. Shelf life is typically 12–24 months unopened.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately after adding BAC water. Use within 30 days for both KLOW and MOTS-C. Never freeze a reconstituted peptide.

Syringe discipline: fresh single-use insulin syringe for every shot. Never reuse. Wipe stopper + injection site with alcohol pad every time.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen, outer thigh, or glute. Rotate sites daily. Never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row.

Week-by-Week
What to Expect Across the 12-Week Run
Weeks 1–2
Settle In · Routine Locks Down

Effects are subtle the first 7–10 days. Sleep tends to deepen — that's usually the first signal both compounds are starting to work. Some mild fatigue is normal as the body adapts to the AMPK signal from MOTS-C and starts the KLOW-driven anti-inflammatory work.

Get the routine locked in: same AM time, MOTS-C first (fasted), KLOW second, wait 30–45 min, eat. Rotate quadrants between the two shots.

Weeks 3–5
Healing + Energy Compound

KLOW's systemic healing signal compounds — any chronic low-grade inflammation calms, gut function improves, skin tone and elasticity lift, connective tissue feels better. MOTS-C's mitochondrial activation lands: cleaner energy across the day, fewer post-meal crashes, better metabolic flexibility.

If you have any joint or soft-tissue issues, this is the window where you'll likely notice them improving.

Weeks 6–8 (KLOW Wraps)
Peak Combined Window · KLOW Vials Empty

The strongest weeks of the combined run. Both compounds at full effect. Energy holds steady, healing benefits consolidate, and the metabolic shift from MOTS-C is fully landed.

KLOW vials empty at end of Week 8. The systemic healing signal stops, but the gains from those 8 weeks continue to remodel for several weeks after.

Weeks 9–12 (MOTS-C Alone)
Metabolic Lock-In

MOTS-C alone M–F AM fasted continues through Week 12 (30 more doses across the second vial). The mitochondrial signal stays running — locks in the metabolic gains, supports any continued fat loss or body comp work, prevents the energy crash that typically follows a deficit phase.

End of cycle: How does sleep feel vs. Week 1? Energy steady? Any healing issues resolved? That answer determines what comes next — another KLOW run, switch to a different healing stack, or maintenance with MOTS-C at lower frequency.

Execution Tips
Fasted Window · Site Rotation · Missed Doses
Tip 01 — The Fasted AM Window
Why Both Compounds Want an Empty Stomach

MOTS-C's AMPK activation is strongest in a fasted state — that's why "AM fasted" matters. The KLOW shot doesn't strictly require fasted, but stacking it in the same window keeps the routine simple and lets the body get both signals before the metabolic day starts.

No food, no calories, no coffee with cream. Black coffee and water are fine. Wait 30–45 minutes after the MOTS-C shot before eating.

Tip 02 — Two Shots, One Window
Site Rotation + Injection Order

Two shots in the same 5-min AM window. Order: MOTS-C first (10u), then KLOW (15u), back-to-back. Use different quadrants of the abdomen for each — e.g. MOTS-C upper right, KLOW lower left. Rotate quadrants daily so no single point takes consecutive shots.

Wipe each spot with an alcohol pad before the shot. Fresh single-use insulin syringe for every injection.

Tip 03 — Missed Doses
What to Do If You Skip

Either compound, M–F window: miss one day, skip it. Don't double up. Resume the next scheduled day. Missing 1–2 doses across the cycle doesn't move the needle.

Missing 3+ days in a row of either compound during the active phase is when the compounding effect starts to thin out — try to keep the routine going M–F.
