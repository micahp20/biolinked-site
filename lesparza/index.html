Lindsay's Mito Repair · Energy · Fat Loss
Repair the engine first. Layer the recovery + cut. Activate the metabolism last for the final push.

Three-phase sequential design built around mitochondrial health. Phase 1 (Wks 1–4): SS-31 alone, M–F, 20 doses — repairs the inner mitochondrial membrane where ATP gets made. Phase 2 (Wks 5–10): GHK-Cu daily + Retatrutide Mon AM 2 mg — recovery + connective tissue layer underneath as Reta starts driving the cut. Phase 3 (Wks 11–16): MOTS-C daily + Reta continues — activates the now-repaired engine via AMPK signaling for the final body comp push.

Why this sequence. Most fat-loss protocols just push the deficit harder. This one is layered. SS-31 fixes the cellular machinery first so the deficit can actually be efficient. GHK-Cu + Reta together let the cut start while recovery + connective tissue stays online. MOTS-C last amplifies metabolic output once Phase 1 has set the foundation and Phase 2 has begun the cut — the final activation push that locks in the body comp shift.

Reta + Cagri run ongoing Wks 5–16 as paired weekly anchors — Reta Monday for the weekly cut, Cagri Friday PM for the weekend brake. Both included across Phase 2 and Phase 3. 2× BAC water included free for reconstitution.

Featured Tips for Your Stack
Tip 01 — Why SS-31 Runs Alone First
The Repair-Before-Activate Logic

Most fat-loss protocols just push the deficit harder. This one starts by fixing the cellular machinery that has to handle the deficit. SS-31 stabilizes the inner mitochondrial membrane — specifically the cardiolipin, which is where the electron transport chain (your ATP factory) lives. When mitochondria are damaged or inefficient, even an aggressive deficit produces slow results + bad energy.

4 weeks of SS-31 alone = the repair signal compounds without other peptides competing for cellular bandwidth. By Week 5, Phase 2 layers in GHK-Cu + Reta on a repaired foundation. Then Phase 3 brings MOTS-C in last (Wks 11–16) to fire AMPK on a system that's now ready for activation. The result: cleaner energy, less crash, less plateau.

Tip 02 — The Weekend Brake (Free Cagri Bonus)
Weekly Friday PM Cagrilintide

Cagri is in your stack for one specific job: protect the weekly deficit Reta is building from getting undone by weekend splurges. Most fat-loss progress disappears on Saturday + Sunday — people are hungrier (you've been in a deficit all week) and social calendars produce calorie spikes. Cagri lands the suppression right when you need it.

How to use it:

  • Take it every Friday evening from Week 5 through Week 16 — NOT before the meal. Before the weekend.
  • 500 mcg = 10 units (0.10 mL). One shot.
  • Effects: dampens hunger 24–48 hrs — covers Sat + Sun. You still enjoy the meals; you just don't overshoot.
  • 12 doses needed across Wks 5–16; vial has 20 doses (8 extra for missed weeks or extension).

The first shot can produce mild nausea or early satiety for 24 hrs. Your first Friday (Wk 5), keep dinner simple — lets first-shot effects wash before higher-stakes weekend meals.

Tip 03 — Daily Logistics by Phase
Mon = Anchor · Fri PM = Brake · Phase 3 AM = Fasted

Phase 2 (Wks 5–10): GHK-Cu M–F AM + Reta Mon AM + Cagri Fri PM. None of these are fasted-dependent.

Week-by-Week
What to Expect Across 16 Weeks
Weeks 1–2 · Phase 1 Start
Subtle Energy Lift · No Scale Movement Yet

SS-31 only, M–F AM — 10u (0.10 mL) = ~1.67 mg. No scale movement — this isn't a weight loss phase. What you might notice: slightly more stable energy through the day, mild improvement in sleep depth, less "afternoon crash" feeling. Mild injection-site redness is normal.

Weeks 3–4 · Phase 1 Wraps
Mito Repair Locked In · Foundation Set

By end of Week 4 the mitochondrial repair is locked in. Energy is more consistent. 20 doses used (~33 mg of the 50 mg vial) — ~10 doses still in the vial as buffer. Phase 2 starts Monday of Week 5.

Weeks 5–6 · Phase 2 Starts
GHK-Cu In, Reta Ramps, Cagri Joins Friday PM

GHK-Cu starts AM M–F. Reta begins Monday AM (2 mg = 20u). Cagri starts Friday PM (500 mcg = 10u) — weekly weekend brake. Expect: appetite suppression from Reta within the first week, mild GI adjustment, GHK-Cu's connective tissue + skin tone effects build through Phase 2, less weekend appetite from Cagri.

Weight starts dropping by end of Week 5 — expect 1–1.5 lbs/wk through these weeks.

Weeks 7–9 · Phase 2 Steady Cut
Body Comp Shift Becomes Visible

Fat loss in this stretch: 1–1.5 lbs/wk steady. Energy stays solid thanks to the Phase 1 mito foundation. GHK-Cu keeps recovery + connective tissue layer working through the deficit. Most clients see the body comp shift in the mirror around Week 8.

Week 10 · GHK-Cu Wraps
GHK-Cu Vial Empties End of Wk 10

GHK-Cu vial empties end of Week 10 (~30 doses at M–F). Reta continues uninterrupted Monday AM. Cagri continues Friday PM. Phase 3 starts Monday of Week 11.

Weeks 11–13 · Phase 3 Starts
MOTS-C Activates, Cut Accelerates

MOTS-C starts AM fasted M–F (10u / 0.10 mL = ~1.33 mg). Reta continues Monday AM (2 mg). Cagri continues Friday PM. Expect: noticeable energy bump from AMPK activation, mild flushing 5–10 min after MOTS-C is normal, deeper sleep in Week 12. Fat-loss curve stays at 1–1.5 lbs/wk — sometimes accelerates here because mito + metabolic activation are stacked.

Weeks 14–16 · Final Stretch
MOTS-C + Reta + Cagri Carry the Close

MOTS-C M–F + Reta Monday + Cagri Friday PM continue at the same cadence. End of Week 16: Phase 3 wraps. MOTS-C has 30-dose buffer (2nd vial unopened — could extend ~6 wks at M–F). Reta has 3-dose buffer (could extend ~3 weeks). Cagri has 8-dose buffer.

End-of-cycle questions: body comp shift vs Week 1? Energy compared to start? How close to goal weight? These shape whether to take 4–8 weeks off, run a maintenance dose, or restart with a different focus.

Tip 04 — Normal Effects
No Action Needed
  • Mild injection-site redness or small bruising — rotate sites, clean technique
  • Mild GI effects first 1–2 weeks of Reta (nausea, early fullness) — subsides as tolerance builds
  • Mild flushing 5–10 min after MOTS-C — normal AMPK vascular response
  • Slightly more vivid dreams in Phase 2 — deeper sleep showing up
  • First-weekend Cagri mild nausea — subsides as tolerance builds
Tip 05 — Watch For
Message BioLinked If Any Of These Happen
  • Injection site hot, swollen, or red >24 hrs — technique or contamination
  • Severe Reta GI past Wk 2 — drop to 0.5 mg (5u) or pause
  • Rapid weight loss >3 lbs/wk sustained — deficit too aggressive, eat more
  • Energy dropping despite Phase 1 + MOTS-C — flag for review
  • Cagri severe nausea / vomiting — drop dose to 250 mcg or pause
