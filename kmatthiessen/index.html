Why it's in your protocol
Glutathione is the body's master intracellular antioxidant — it's what your cells actually use to neutralize reactive oxygen species, recycle vitamins C and E, and drive Phase II liver detoxification. Oral glutathione is largely destroyed in the gut, which is why subcutaneous delivery at 180mg is a meaningful step up from supplements. For skin specifically, glutathione inhibits tyrosinase (the enzyme that drives melanin production in response to oxidative stress), which is why consistent dosing produces a visible evening of tone and brightness over 4–8 weeks. Mon/Fri dosing spaces out the 180mg bolus efficiently — higher, less frequent doses outperform daily micro-doses for systemic glutathione because of how it's distributed and reused across tissues.

Why it's in your protocol
CJC-1295/Ipamorelin is the gold-standard "GH pulse" peptide combo — and it works by two complementary mechanisms. CJC-1295 is a GHRH (growth hormone releasing hormone) analog that tells the pituitary to produce more GH. Ipamorelin is a selective ghrelin receptor agonist that triggers the pituitary to release stored GH in a clean, pulsatile pattern — with minimal cortisol or prolactin spillover that older GHRPs caused. Combined, they amplify your body's own natural nighttime GH pulse. Taken pre-bed on an empty stomach, this drives deeper slow-wave (Stage 3/4) sleep, overnight cellular repair, and gradual improvements in body composition, skin quality, and recovery. Critically, this is not exogenous HGH — your pituitary is still in control, which is why it's far safer and doesn't shut down your own axis.
