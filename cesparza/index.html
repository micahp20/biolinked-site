Storage & Handling
Keep It Cold · Keep It Clean
❄ Refrigerated, Protected, Rotated

Dry (lyophilized) vials: refrigerated at 36–46°F before reconstitution. Protect from light. Never freeze.

Reconstituted vials: refrigerate immediately. Use within 30 days. Fresh insulin syringe for each draw — never reuse.

Site rotation: SubQ abdomen rotates between 4 quadrants. Never inject the same 1″ spot two days in a row. During Phase 1 M–F use different quadrants for BPC AM and CJC/Ipa PM (e.g., BPC AM upper-right, CJC/Ipa PM upper-left). BPC site-targeting: for a sore joint or tendon, you can shoot the BPC closer to the affected area (still SubQ, just nearer) for a stronger local effect.

Featured Tips for Your Stack
Tip 01 — Why BPC + CJC/Ipa Run Together
Tissue Fix + GH Pulse = The Recovery Bottleneck Broken

The actual problem isn't lifting harder. It's that the soft tissue + nervous system can't catch up between sessions. Two compounds, two jobs:

  • BPC-157 (AM, M–F) heals the soft tissue, tendons, joint capsules, gut lining. The compound that directly attacks the soreness + lagging recovery.
  • CJC-1295/Ipamorelin (PM before bed, M–F) pulses GH during slow-wave sleep — where the actual recovery and IGF-1 signaling for muscle protein synthesis happens.

Run them together for 8 weeks M–F = tissue heals while sleep deepens and lean mass starts climbing. Sat/Sun off lets the body integrate. By Week 4–5 the soreness gap closes. Then Phase 2 (KLOW, 4 weeks M–F) keeps the recovery wrap on while the gains consolidate and you push the nutrition + training side.

Tip 02 — Protein Intake (Required for the Recomp)
1 g per lb Body Weight · Non-Negotiable

Peptides build capacity. Food builds mass. You're gaining 20 lbs of lean tissue — the GH pulse from CJC/Ipa only translates to muscle if there's protein substrate to build it from.

  • Target: 1 g protein per lb body weight. Start at 185 g/day, ramp toward 205–220 g/day as bodyweight climbs.
  • Distribution: 4–5 protein-heavy meals across the day, 40–50 g per meal. Body can't use a single 200 g protein meal — spread it.
  • Post-workout: whey shake within 60 min of training. Fast-digesting protein when muscle protein synthesis is most receptive.
  • Pre-bed: slow-digest protein (cottage cheese, casein shake, Greek yogurt) 1.5–2 hrs before the CJC/Ipa shot. NOT closer than 1.5 hrs — insulin near the GH pulse blunts the signal.

If hitting these numbers feels like a chore, the next tip is your answer.

Tip 03 — Weight Gainer (If Calorie Surplus Is the Bottleneck)
Mass-Gainer Shake — The Surplus Cheat Code

Hitting a calorie surplus while doing 185 lbs of bodyweight of protein is genuinely hard. Small appetite + busy days + the appetite-suppressing effect of intense training = most people undershoot the surplus and wonder why the scale isn't moving.

Solution: a quality mass-gainer shake (500–800 cal, ~50 g protein + slow carbs like oats / sweet potato powder + healthy fats). One shake replaces a full meal you don't have time to eat OR sits between meals as a calorie anchor.

  • Best timing: post-workout (carbs + protein into the open window) OR mid-afternoon between meals 2 and 3.
  • What to look for: >40 g protein, real carb sources (oats, maltodextrin OK in moderation), avoid mass-gainers that are mostly sugar.
  • Quick DIY option: whey + oats + banana + peanut butter + milk = ~600–700 cal mass-gainer. Cheaper than commercial.

Not required if your appetite + meal timing already lets you hit 3,500+ cal/day with quality food. Required if it doesn't.

Tip 04 — MK-677 (Ibutamoren) Recommendation
The Optional Next-Add · Oral GH Secretagogue

MK-677 is an oral GH secretagogue (no injection). Stacks particularly well with CJC/Ipa in Phase 1 and especially with KLOW in Phase 2 maintenance. Not in the bundle — this is the recommended next-add if you want to compound the effect.

What it does:

  • Deepens sleep — the biggest recovery lever, on top of the CJC/Ipa effect.
  • Increases appetite — directly helps the calorie surplus problem above.
  • Sustained GH/IGF-1 baseline lift — pushes the recovery + lean mass signal beyond the CJC/Ipa pulse.

Dosing: 10–25 mg PO once daily, typically before bed. Start at 10 mg for 1–2 weeks, titrate up to 20–25 mg if tolerated.

Side notes: water retention is common (you'll look "fuller" within a week — that's water, not muscle, week 1). If appetite spikes too hard, drop to 10 mg or move dose to morning. Mild tingling/numbness in hands is occasionally reported (carpal-tunnel-adjacent) — resolves on stopping or dropping dose. Not for use during fasted training blocks (appetite spike).

Tip 05 — AM + PM Logistics (Phase 1 M–F)
2 Shots a Day · ~2 Minutes Each · Sat/Sun Off

Phase 1 has two daily injection windows on Mon–Fri only: AM (1 shot) and PM bedtime (1 shot). Total ~4 min/day. Weekends off entirely.

AM (after waking):

  • BPC 10u abdomen quadrant A (e.g., upper-right). Doesn't need fasted.
  • Continue your morning routine.

PM (bedtime, 1.5–2 hrs after last food):

  • CJC/Ipa 10u abdomen quadrant B (e.g., upper-left). Different quadrant from AM.
  • Lights out within 15 min — you want the GH pulse landing during slow-wave sleep.

Sat + Sun: no shots. Body integrates. Phase 2: single KLOW shot M–F AM. Sat/Sun off. Simpler still.

Weeks 1–2 · Phase 1 Starts

BPC AM M–F + CJC/Ipa PM before bed M–F. First thing you'll notice: deeper sleep within 5–7 days. Vivid dreams are a tell-sign the GH pulse is landing during slow-wave sleep. Soreness from training starts dropping noticeably by end of Week 2. Scale may not move yet — lean mass takes longer than sleep effects. Sat/Sun off — this is the rhythm for all 8 weeks.

Weeks 3–4 · Recovery Gap Closes
Workouts Stop Compounding Soreness

By Week 3–4 the post-workout soreness gap should close meaningfully — you're recovering between sessions instead of staying sore. If you're hitting protein + surplus targets, scale starts climbing 0.5–1 lb/wk lean. If scale isn't moving, the bottleneck is nutrition (not the peptides) — revisit the protein + mass-gainer tips.

Weeks 5–6 · Mid-Phase 1
Lean Mass Trend Set · Strength Climbing

You're now halfway through Phase 1. Sleep depth is the new baseline. Strength in the gym should be ticking up — better recovery means more available work capacity. Scale on track for 0.5–1 lb/wk lean if nutrition is dialed. Keep the M–F rhythm; weekends off keep the system from desensitizing.

Weeks 7–8 · Phase 1 Wraps
Bottles Done End of Week 8

BPC and CJC/Ipa bottles run out at end of Week 8 (40 doses each, exact fit). Expected progress: meaningful drop in post-workout soreness, deeper sleep is the new baseline, scale up 4–8 lbs lean if nutrition is on. Phase 2 (KLOW) starts Monday of Week 9.

Weeks 9–12 · Phase 2 KLOW Maintenance
Recovery Wrap On · Nutrition Carries the Mass

KLOW M–F AM only. One shot/day. The recovery + connective tissue signal stays on while the gains from Phase 1 consolidate. This is where nutrition does the heavy lifting — keep protein + surplus dialed and the scale should keep climbing. KLOW bottle empties exactly at end of Week 12 (20 doses).

Optional add-on: if you started MK-677 in Phase 1 or are starting it now, this is the window where its appetite + sleep effects compound the hardest.

End of Week 12 · Cycle Assessment
Where Are You vs 185 → 205?

Realistic Week-12 weight if nutrition is on: 195–200 lbs lean. Hitting 205 is a 6–10 week extension of the surplus + training, not a single-cycle thing. Questions to ask: soreness vs Week 1? Sleep quality? Strength in the gym? These shape whether to: rerun a cycle (add a second BPC + CJC/Ipa block), shift to a pure KLOW maintenance block while pushing nutrition, or layer in MK-677 + GHK-Cu for a longer recomp tail.

Tip 06 — Normal Effects
No Action Needed
  • Mild injection-site redness or small bruising — rotate sites, clean technique
  • Vivid dreams from CJC/Ipa — deeper sleep showing up, this is the signal you want
  • Mild head pressure / "fullness" first 1–2 weeks of CJC/Ipa — subsides as tolerance builds
  • Slight water retention starting Week 2 — GH effect, normal
  • Increased appetite as Phase 1 progresses — lean into it, you need the surplus
  • Tingling at BPC injection site for a few minutes — normal
Tip 07 — Watch For
Message BioLinked If Any Of These Happen
  • Injection site hot, swollen, or red >24 hrs — technique or contamination
  • Persistent numbness or tingling in hands (if running MK-677) — drop MK dose or pause
  • Significant water retention / puffy face — back off MK-677 if running it; otherwise flag
  • Sleep disturbance — should be deepening, not worsening; flag if reversed
  • No recovery improvement by Week 3 — flag, may need dosing/site adjustment
  • Scale not moving despite hitting protein + 500 cal surplus — flag for nutrition review
