Tip 01 — Why Pinealon + Epitalon Are the TOP of Your Stack
The Pineal Pair · Synergy for Sleep + Stress at the Root

You said your biggest goal was sleep + stress. Pinealon + Epitalon are the most direct lever in this entire stack for that goal — and they work synergistically because they target the same gland from two different angles.

Pinealon (Khavinson tripeptide) targets the pineal gland's acute function — neuroprotection, stress resilience (anxiolytic without sedation), cognitive sharpness, and resistance to oxidative damage in the brain. Effect builds over weeks of consistent dosing.

Epitalon (Khavinson tetrapeptide) targets the pineal gland's systemic restoration — normalizes melatonin output, restores circadian rhythm (when you fall asleep, when you wake), and activates telomerase (cellular longevity signal). Famously called the "anti-aging pineal peptide."

Why they're better together than either alone:

  • Pinealon = stress resilience + cognition + neuroprotection (acute pineal support)
  • Epitalon = melatonin output + circadian rhythm + telomerase (systemic pineal restoration)
  • Together = both layers of pineal function get addressed in a single 20-day M–F course

Why this is the TOP of your stack at 57 with sleep 2/5: Most sleep stacks work downstream — they manage symptoms (trazodone shuts off arousal for that one night). Pinealon + Epitalon work upstream — they restore the gland that produces melatonin and regulates the entire sleep-wake cycle.
