Notes & Tips
Reference
Tip 01 — Thyroid Spacing
Levo + Lio Away from Multi & Psyllium

Levothyroxine + Liothyronine taken on a wake-fasted stomach with water only, then a 1-hour buffer before the morning drink (psyllium husk binds thyroid hormone) and a longer gap before the noon multivitamin (calcium / iron / magnesium block T4 absorption). Maintaining the spacing is the single biggest lever on stable TSH.

Tip 02 — PM Pre-Bed Fast
Last Food 1.5–2 hrs Before CJC/Ipa

Food — especially carbs and protein — spikes insulin. Insulin shuts down GH release at the pituitary. A fed CJC/Ipa shot is wasted. Dinner → wait 1.5–2 hrs → brush teeth → inject → lights out within 30 min. Pair with the pre-bed supplement stack (apigenin / theanine / mag glycinate / taurine / glycine).

Tip 03 — NAD Slow Push
15–30 Seconds, Not 3

NAD SubQ can cause a brief warmth / flushing in chest, neck, or face during or right after the shot — harmless, clears in 1–5 min. Push the plunger slowly over 15–30 seconds to minimize it. If still uncomfortable, slow it further or split the dose (half → pause 10 sec → half).

Tip 04 — Ongoing vs Cycled
What Continues Past Jul 6

The 9-compound peptide cycle wraps at the Jul 6 cycle end — SLU, Cerebrolysin, Humanin, Testegen, Glutathione, NAD+, Melanotan, CJC/Ipa, GHK-Cu all stop or reassess at that point. TRT and Retatrutide are ongoing — they continue at the same cadence (M/W/F for TRT, M/Th for Reta) beyond the cycle end as the maintenance layer.
